🗺️ Your 3-Day Goa Adventure

Perfect blend of beaches, culture, and relaxation in beautiful Goa!

📅 Day 1: South Goa Beaches

🌅 Morning: Arrive & Palolem Beach

Fly into Goa airport, taxi to Palolem Beach (1.5 hours, ₹1500). Relax on the pristine golden sands and settle into beachside accommodation.

🍽️ Lunch: Beach Shack Experience

Fresh seafood at Palolem beach shacks. Try Goan fish curry rice (₹400-600).

🌞 Afternoon: Palolem to Patnem Beach Walk

Scenic 20-minute coastal walk to quieter Patnem Beach. Perfect for swimming and photography.

🌙 Evening: Sunset & Local Dinner

Watch stunning sunset from Palolem, then dinner at local restaurant. Try prawn balchao (₹600-800).

Estimated Daily Budget: ₹3,000

📅 Day 2: North Goa Exploration

🌅 Morning: Travel to North Goa

Taxi to Calangute area (2.5 hours, ₹2500). Check into accommodation and explore Calangute Beach's lively atmosphere.

🍽️ Lunch: Calangute Beachfront

Variety of restaurants with beach views. Multi-cuisine options (₹500-700).

🌞 Afternoon: Baga Beach & Water Sports

Short taxi ride to Baga Beach. Try parasailing or jet skiing. Great for adventure seekers.

🌙 Evening: Fort Aguada Sunset

Historic Portuguese fort with panoramic Arabian Sea views. Perfect for sunset photos. Return for dinner in Baga.

Estimated Daily Budget: ₹4,000

📅 Day 3: Heritage & Departure

🌅 Morning: Old Goa Churches

UNESCO World Heritage sites - Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Rich Portuguese colonial history (30 mins from North Goa).

🍽️ Lunch: Old Goa Local Cuisine

Traditional Goan restaurant near the churches. Try bebinca dessert (₹400-500).

🌞 Afternoon: Airport Departure

Taxi to airport (1 hour, ₹800). Flight departure with amazing Goan memories!

Estimated Daily Budget: ₹1,500

Total Trip Budget: ₹8,500 (excluding accommodation and flights)
